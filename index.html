“Be the change that you wish to see in the world” - Mahatma Gandhi
Are you looking for a platform to show your observational and problem solving skills? Do it in real time. Travel around Kochi, get acquainted to real life problems you’ll face and create a solution. Unravel the mysteries that will lead the victorious team to glory, accolades and attractive prizes.


  • 15 finalist teams will have to complete various tasks at different locations

  • There would be three rounds of Elimination Process of which 6 Best Teams goes for the final treasure hunt.

  • A Team of 4 (1 Team Captain, 3 Members) is required for a team to participate.

  • Multiple Teams from a college can participate

  • College ID Card/Permission Letter necessary for the participation.

  • The Team should be ready to sign a indemnity before the event.

  • The Registration Charges of Rs.1000/team has to be paid at the counter

  1. The Team That Stays Together. Your team must stay together at all times. You may not split up to “divide” tasks.

  2. Playing Fair. Have fun and try to win. But don’t let your competitive instincts drive you to ruin other people’s chances to win. If you do, your team will be disqualified. That means no sabotaging, lying, cheating, stealing, or copying other people’s work.

  3. Cheaters Never Win. If anyone on your team cheats or breaks the rules, the whole team gets disqualified.

  4. Some of the events are physically demanding and require fair amount of travelling. Only those participants who can handle the stress of a day of continuous activity are advised to take part.

  5. Each task will have a separate set of instructions which will be handed to each team at the task location.

  6. How to Win - Only teams that complete a task will be ranked for calculating the points to be awarded for each task.

  7. If You Fight the Law (and the Law Wins). You’re here to have fun, but to do so legally. We don’t want to get sued. So here it is. If you break any laws at any time during any portion of the All Kochi Treasure hunt, your team will be disqualified. This includes, but is not limited to, moving violations, trespassing, burglary, larceny, embezzlement, grand theft, petty theft, shoplifting, assault, plagiarism, blackmail, indecent exposure, decent exposure, recent exposure, having a concealed firearm, unlawfully discharging a firearm, unlawful sale of a firearm, brandishing a weapon, bribery, forgery, perjury, fraud, computer fraud, mail fraud, telemarketing fraud, tax evasion, tax fraud, insurance fraud, securities fraud, and bad hair. Seriously, if you get so much as a speeding ticket, you lose.

  8. Appeals. There is no appeals process in All Kochi Treasure hunt.

  9. Judges. The judges will be judging you. We will be judging you since the moment you get here.

  10. Late Loses. Your team, in its entirety, must return to the specific location no later than the specified time as specified by the judges. The judge’s requirements in this area will be specific.

  11. Decisions . All decisions by the judges are final. No exceptions.

  12. Strangers. Some tasks will with deal with strangers. It is important to be kind and respectful with strangers and with that always be on guard and stay safe, use your healthy judgment when it comes to people who you don’t know.

  13. Citizens. No harassing citizens of your town, physically or verbally for the items or photographers. It is their right not to help you and you should respect that(even if you think they are lame for not helping you.)

  14. Teams must be courteous and respectful to their teammates and all outside people involved in the Treasure Hunt.

  15. If businesses or private individuals lend you items its okay, but please note that it is your responsibility to return any borrowed items to their original owners.

  16. KNOW YOUR BOUNDARIES: Everyone needs to know the boundaries of the All KochiTreasure Hunt. Make it very clear, what they are, whether you are inside, outside, in a certain neighborhood etc.

  17. NO LITTERING. Always clean up after yourselves and your teammates.

  18. ONLY VEHICLES APPROVED BY THE COORDINATORS IS ALLOWED..!! If you are strategic enough you can complete the Great Treasure Hunt at a minimal cost!

  19. Judges determinations after the Great treasure hunt are final.
