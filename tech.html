Gear Train

Number of Participants   2

Gear train is a mechanical system formed by mounting gears on a frame so the teeth of the gears engage. Gear teeth are designed to ensure the pitch circles of engaging gears roll on each other without slipping, providing a smooth transmission of rotation from one gear to the next.
In this competition , a team consisting of two members will be provided with a set of gears and shaft fixed on board, team within alloted time have to logically arrange the gears and have to successfully give an output such as a wieght should be lifted, bursting a balloon, ringing a bell.There are more than one output.The team can mesh the gears to give more than one output.The team which successfully gives more output within alloted time will be declared as winners.
The competition is conducted in two stages

  1. Preliminary stage: In this stage, a team is provided with set of plastic gears along with a small board.The members should arrange the gears accordingly to give a specific output.Only the top 20 teams will qualify for main stage.

  2. Main stage : Here the team is provided with set of metal gears and shafts fixed on a large wooden board.The team can arrange the gears in any way to accomplish the task.The finishing time is noted and accordingly the winners are declared.


  • A team mainly consists of two members .

  • The decision of judges will be final.

  • All participants must carry their college ID cards along with them.
