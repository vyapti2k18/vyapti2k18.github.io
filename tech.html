  1. The Team That Stays Together. Your team must stay together at all times. You may not split up to “divide” tasks.

  3. Cheaters Never Win. If anyone on your team cheats or breaks the rules, the whole team gets disqualified.

If you break any laws at any time during any portion of the All Kochi Treasure hunt, your team will be disqualified. This includes, but is not limited to, moving violations, trespassing, burglary, larceny, embezzlement, grand theft, petty theft, shoplifting, assault, plagiarism, blackmail, indecent exposure, decent exposure, recent exposure, having a concealed firearm, unlawfully discharging a firearm, unlawful sale of a firearm, brandishing a weapon, bribery, forgery, perjury, fraud, computer fraud, mail fraud, telemarketing fraud, tax evasion, tax fraud, insurance fraud, securities fraud, and bad hair. Seriously, if you get so much as a speeding ticket, you lose.

  8. Appeals. There is no appeals process in All Kochi Treasure hunt.

  9. Judges. The judges will be judging you. We will be judging you since the moment you get here.

  10. Late Loses. Your team, in its entirety, must return to the specific location no later than the specified time as specified by the judges. The judge’s requirements in this area will be specific.

  11. Decisions . All decisions by the judges are final. No exceptions.

  12. Strangers. Some tasks will with deal with strangers. It is important to be kind and respectful with strangers and with that always be on guard and stay safe, use your healthy judgment when it comes to people who you don’t know.

  13. Citizens. No harassing citizens of your town, physically or verbally for the items or photographers. It is their right not to help you and you should respect that(even if you think they are lame for not helping you.)

  14. Teams must be courteous and respectful to their teammates and all outside people involved in the Treasure Hunt.

  15. If businesses or private individuals lend you items its okay, but please note that it is your responsibility to return any borrowed items to their original owners.

  16. KNOW YOUR BOUNDARIES: Everyone needs to know the boundaries of the All KochiTreasure Hunt. Make it very clear, what they are, whether you are inside, outside, in a certain neighborhood etc.

  17. NO LITTERING. Always clean up after yourselves and your teammates.

  18. ONLY VEHICLES APPROVED BY THE COORDINATORS IS ALLOWED..!! If you are strategic enough you can complete the Great Treasure Hunt at a minimal cost!

Gear Train

Number of Participants   2

Gear train is a mechanical system formed by mounting gears on a frame so the teeth of the gears engage. Gear teeth are designed to ensure the pitch circles of engaging gears roll on each other without slipping, providing a smooth transmission of rotation from one gear to the next.
In this competition , a team consisting of two members will be provided with a set of gears and shaft fixed on board, team within alloted time have to logically arrange the gears and have to successfully give an output such as a wieght should be lifted, bursting a balloon, ringing a bell.There are more than one output.The team can mesh the gears to give more than one output.The team which successfully gives more output within alloted time will be declared as winners.
The competition is conducted in two stages

  1. Preliminary stage: In this stage, a team is provided with set of plastic gears along with a small board.The members should arrange the gears accordingly to give a specific output.Only the top 20 teams will qualify for main stage.

  2. Main stage : Here the team is provided with set of metal gears and shafts fixed on a large wooden board.The team can arrange the gears in any way to accomplish the task.The finishing time is noted and accordingly the winners are declared.


  • A team mainly consists of two members .

  • The decision of judges will be final.

  • All participants must carry their college ID cards along with them.
